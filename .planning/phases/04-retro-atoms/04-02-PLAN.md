<objective>
Build the data family: the `useTableSelection` hook (the critical TUI-06 correctness primitive — id-keyed anchor+range selection that survives table re-sort/re-filter), `RetroPagination` (sketch-008 beveled pager), and `RetroTabs` (folder-tab idiom with roving tabindex). This plan does NOT wire selection into the Bottombar — that is consumer-side wiring shown in RESEARCH; the hook just exposes `{ selected, onRowClick, clear }` and the demo/feature pages register the bulk-action group.

Purpose: Selection is the single most failure-prone primitive (Pitfall 1 — index-based ranges silently re-select wrong rows after sort). Landing the id-keyed hook with its sort-survival test from the first commit guards the contract. Pagination + tabs round out the data family every list page consumes.
Output: `retro/data/{useTableSelection.ts, RetroPagination.tsx, RetroTabs.tsx}` + tests + subdir barrel.
